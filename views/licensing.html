Key Florida Business Licenses

Professional handling of all your licensing needs

FDACS License

Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services

What It Covers

Food licensing, food establishment permits, food service operations, catering businesses, food manufacturing, and food processing.

Who Needs It

  • Restaurants
  • Catering companies
  • Food manufacturers
  • Commissary kitchens
  • Food trucks
  • Bakeries

Key Requirements

  • Food Handler Certification
  • ServSafe Manager Certification
  • HACCP plan (high-risk foods)
  • Facility documentation

Processing Timeline

Typically 2-4 weeks after submission, depending on inspection requirements.

DBPR License

Division of Businesses and Professional Regulation

What It Covers

Food service licenses, public lodging licenses, business regulation, and professional licensing for various industries.

Who Needs It

  • Food service businesses
  • Beverages and alcohol
  • DFS regulated businesses
  • Professional services
  • Food service retailers
  • Hospitality services

Key Requirements

  • Business entity documentation
  • Ownership information
  • Facility location proof
  • Application fee payment

Processing Timeline

Typically 1-3 weeks after application submission.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common licensing questions

Do I need both FDACS and DBPR licenses?

Not always. It depends on your business type. Most food-related businesses need at least one. We'll evaluate your specific situation and advise accordingly.

How long does the licensing process take?

Typically 2-6 weeks depending on your business type and the completeness of your application. We expedite the process wherever possible.

What happens if my application is denied?

We work with you to address any concerns and resubmit. Most denials are resolved with additional documentation or minor modifications.

Can you help with license renewals?

Absolutely! We handle all renewal paperwork and ensure you never miss critical deadlines for license maintenance.

What if I'm opening a food truck?

Food trucks require special consideration for both FDACS and DBPR. We manage the entire process including commissary coordination and mobile vendor permits.

Are there industry-specific licenses I need?

Yes. Depending on your business (catering, alcohol, etc.), additional licenses may be required. We provide a complete assessment of all necessary permits.
